What are the phases of agile development


CONCEPT

During the first step of the agile software development life cycle, the team plans and prioritizes projects.


INCEPTION

Once you have identified the project, work with stakeholders to determine requirements.


ITERATION

Once a team has defined the requirements for the initial sprint based on stakeholder feedback and requirements, work begins.


RELEASE

Fix any defects


MAINTENACE

During the retirement phase, you remove the system version from production, typically when you want to replace a system with a new version.


RETIREMENT

This phase involves ongoing support for the software version.
